High Accuracy Model

Our Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is specifically tailored for the subtle patterns, colors, and scale arrangements unique to **Indian snakes**, minimizing misidentification risks which are common with generic global models.

  • **Big Four Coverage**: Cobra, Krait, Russell's Viper, Saw Scaled Viper.
